Frequently Asked Questions

What fitness level do I need?
Moderate fitness is sufficient for most treks. You should be able to walk 5–7 hours daily. Prior trekking experience is helpful but not mandatory.
When is the best time to trek?
Spring (Mar–May) and Autumn (Sep–Nov) offer the most stable weather and clear mountain views. Winter is good for lower altitude treks.
Do I need a guide and porter?
Yes, it is mandatory to have a licensed guide for trekking in Nepal. Porters are optional but highly recommended to enhance your experience.
What is included in the trek price?
Accommodation, meals during trek, permits, TIMS card, licensed guide, porters, and all land transportation. International flights are not included.
How do I book a trek?
You can book directly through our website, or contact us via email/phone. A 20% deposit is required to confirm your booking.
