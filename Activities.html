Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum age for paragliding?
Minimum age is 5 years for tandem flights with a parent/guardian. Solo flights require a valid license.
Is previous experience required for rafting?
No experience needed for class II–III rapids. Our guides provide full safety briefing and training.
What should I wear for canyoning?
Quick‑dry sportswear, secure footwear (water shoes or old sneakers), and a change of clothes. We provide wetsuits and helmets.
Are activities available year‑round?
Most activities run year‑round, but some (like rafting) are best during post‑monsoon (Oct–Dec) and spring (Mar–May). Check individual pages for details.
What is your safety record?
We maintain a perfect safety record with certified guides, modern equipment, and strict adherence to international standards.
