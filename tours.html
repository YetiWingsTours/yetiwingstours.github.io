Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best time for cultural tours in Nepal?
The best time is during spring (March–May) and autumn (September–November) when the weather is pleasant and skies are clear.
Are international tours guided?
Yes, all our international tours include experienced local guides who provide in-depth cultural and historical insights.
What is included in the tour price?
Accommodation, transportation, guided tours, entrance fees, and select meals. International flights are not included.
Can I customize a tour package?
Absolutely! We can tailor any tour to match your preferences, duration, and budget. Contact us for a custom quote.
How do I book a multi-country tour?
You can book online or contact our travel specialists. We’ll help you design the perfect multi-country itinerary.
