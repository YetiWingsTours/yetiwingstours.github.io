About Yeti Wings

Your trusted partner for Himalayan adventures since 2025

Our Story

Yeti Wings Tours & Travels was founded in 2025 by Chetnath Ghimire, a passionate tourism professional with deep roots in the Himalayas. Born and raised in the shadow of the mountains, Chetnath grew up with a profound love for Nepal's natural beauty and cultural heritage.

The name "Yeti Wings" symbolizes our mission: to help travelers spread their wings and explore the mystical Himalayas, just like the legendary Yeti that roams these peaks. We believe that every journey should feel like flying – free, exhilarating, and unforgettable.

What started as a dream to share Nepal's wonders with the world has grown into a trusted travel company serving adventurers from across the globe. Today, we pride ourselves on creating authentic experiences that go beyond typical tourism, connecting travelers with the real heart of Nepal.
