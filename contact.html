Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a trip with Yeti Wings?
Book directly online, use the form above, or call/WhatsApp us. We'll respond within 24h.
What payment methods do you accept?
Bank transfer, cards, PayPal, eSewa, cash. 30% deposit to confirm.
Can I customize a tour or trek?
Absolutely! Contact us with your requirements for a personalized itinerary.
What is your cancellation policy?
Free up to 30 days before. Within 30 days fees may apply. Travel insurance recommended.
Do I need travel insurance?
Yes, mandatory for treks. Must cover medical, evacuation, trip cancellation.
How far in advance should I book?
Peak seasons: 3-6 months. Other times: 1-2 months. Last-minute? Contact us.
