About YetiWings Tours

Your trusted partner for unforgettable adventures in the Himalayas and beyond

Our Story

Founded in 2024, YetiWings Tours and Travels has been providing exceptional travel experiences throughout Nepal and beyond. What started as a small family-operated business has grown into a reputable travel company known for its personalized service and attention to detail.

Our name "YetiWings" symbolizes our commitment to helping you reach new heights in your travel adventures, just like the mythical Yeti roaming the Himalayas and wings carrying you to new destinations.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create unforgettable travel experiences that showcase the natural beauty, rich culture, and warm hospitality of Nepal. We strive to make adventure travel accessible, sustainable, and transformative for our clients.

We believe in responsible tourism that benefits local communities while minimizing environmental impact. All our tours are designed with sustainability in mind, supporting local economies and preserving cultural heritage.

Why Choose Us

  • Expert Local Knowledge: Our team consists of experienced travel professionals who know Nepal intimately.
  • Personalized Service: We tailor every itinerary to match your interests, pace, and budget.
  • Safety First: Your safety is our top priority. All our guides are trained in first aid and emergency procedures.
  • Sustainable Tourism: We're committed to ethical practices that support local communities and protect the environment.
  • 24/7 Support: Our team is available round the clock to assist you during your travels.
